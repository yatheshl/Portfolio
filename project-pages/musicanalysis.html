Statistical Music Analysis
Github
Analysis of Music Trends project is an in-depth examination of the patterns and trends prevalent in the music industry. This data-driven research project utilizes statistical analysis techniques to explore the various factors that have influenced the popularity and evolution of music over time. By delving into aspects such as genre preferences, artist influences, and listener demographics, the study aims to provide valuable insights for musicians, producers, and the music industry as a whole.
Research Questions
1. Extract lyrics data for top rap songs from Billboard charts (2013-2022) to analyze and visualize trends in popular rap lyrics over time.
2. Identify the most popular songs in major US metropolitan cities from October 2021 to March 2023 and analyze regional music preferences.
3. Investigate the relationship between tempo and energy of top 50 popular rap and pop songs from Billboard charts using Spotipy.
4. Perform sentiment analysis on tweets and song lyrics of Kanye West to understand the correlation between his social media presence and music.
5. Analyze sampling and production trends in the rap industry using data from Whosampled and Billboard, and create a graph connecting artists and producers.
